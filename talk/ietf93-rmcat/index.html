Everything in the world by nature is simple, it is those clever people who make it complicated. This is Safiqul Islam, who believes in simplicity (yet considered as an ultimate complexity), a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Networks and Distributed Systems Group, at the Department of Informatics, University of Oslo. I am working with Professor Michael Welzl and Professor Stein Gjessing. I am active in the IETF and IRTF where I have contributed to several IETF/IRTF Working Groups.
